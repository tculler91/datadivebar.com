## What Is This Place?

Data Dive Bar is an independent data, analytics, and culture blog with the soul of a sticky-floored dive bar and the brain of a stats department that got kicked out of academia. 

We publish data-driven stories, interactive visualizations, sports analysis, and the occasional piece that probably shouldn't exist but does anyway. Because we do what we want, and if you don't like it, you can 'git' out!
