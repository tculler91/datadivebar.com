How H2H Scoring Works

Forget RBIs. Forget ERA. In the Fantasy Vibes League, your players score on the Brofessor's proprietary metrics. Draft 5 batters and 5 pitchers. Each matchup compares 10 categories head-to-head. Win more categories, win the matchup. All stats are usage-weighted — you can't game the system with a reliever who threw 12 innings all year.

Batting Categories (5)

SAUCE = OPS × AVG × PA
BEEF = SLG × (wt/180)^1.5 × AB/100
VAR = HR × OPS × (wt/200) × √PA
DAWG = RBI × OPS × (wt/ht) × G/10
YOLO = TB × SLG × √PA

Pitching Categories (5)

FILTH = (K/9 × 100 / ERA) × √IP
MENACE = (SO-BB) × √IP / WHIP
ICE = (SO/BB) × √IP / ERA
BYRD = IP × (9/BB9) × (1/ERA) × (9/K9)
MOYER = IP × (Age/25) × (9/ERA) / K9
