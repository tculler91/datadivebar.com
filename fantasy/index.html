H2H Category Scoring

Each week you go head-to-head against one opponent across 10 proprietary categories. Win more categories, win the week. Draft 5 batters and 5 pitchers. All stats are volume-weighted — workhorses beat one-inning wonders.

Batting Categories (5)

VAR = (HR × OPS × (wt/180)²) × √(AB/400)
BEEF = (SLG × TB) × (wt/180)^1.2 / 100
SAUCE = (AVG × HR × OPS) × (AB/400) × 30
GRIT = ((RBI + SB×3) × H × AVG) / max(HR,1) × (AB/500) / 5
JUICE = (TB × OPS × HR) / 50 × √(AB/400)

Pitching Categories (5)

FILTH = (K/9 × IP) / max(ERA,0.5) / 8
ICE = (SO / max(BB,1)) × IP / max(ERA,0.5) / 10
MENACE = (SO−BB) × IP / max(WHIP,0.5) / 800
BYRD = (IP × SO/max(BB,1)) / max(K/9,3) / 5
MOYER = (IP × Age × 9/max(ERA,0.5)) / max(K/9,3) / 50

BYRD Index: Named for Paul Byrd, who threw 83 mph with a delivery like he was checking his blind spot. Rewards craft over stuff.
MOYER Score: Named for Jamie Moyer, who threw 78 mph changeups until age 49. Rewards elder statesmen who defy time.
