How Scoring Works

Forget RBIs. Forget ERA. In the Fantasy Vibes League, your players score on the Brofessor's proprietary metrics. Draft 5 batters and 3 pitchers. Highest combined VIBE SCORE wins.

Batter Scoring

VAR = (HR × OPS × weight²) / 1000
BEEF = (SLG × 1000) × (weight/180)^1.5
SAUCE = AVG × HR × OPS × 100

Batter Score = VAR + BEEF + SAUCE

Pitcher Scoring

FILTH = (K/9 × 100) / max(ERA, 0.5)
MENACE = (SO-BB) × IP × (1/max(WHIP,0.5))
ICE = (SO/max(BB,1)) × (1/max(ERA,0.5)) × IP

Pitcher Score = (FILTH + ICE) × 2 + MENACE/50
